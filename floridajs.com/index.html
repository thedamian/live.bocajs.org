It's all about you

The FloridaJS group is here to demonstrate the power of the great resources South Florida has for the world.

In addition to vanilla java script, we'll be looking at frameworks such as ReactJS, NodeJS, AngularJS (1, 1.5 AND 2, 4,5,6,7,8,9...), Ember.js, Ionic and YES even the KING: JQuery!!!!

FloridaJS meets the 1st Tuesday of each month at Cendyn Spaces and online at Youtube.com/FloridaJS & on Twitch.tv/FloridaJS. We have great membersfrom beginner to advanced.
